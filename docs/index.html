Features

What's in the build

The whole feature set, plainly listed — no upsells gating the basics.

✓

Foreground capture

While the app is open, anything you copy from another app is captured automatically — no setup, no signing in.

☆

Pin what matters

Star entries to protect them from the 500-cap eviction. Pinned items always sort to the top.

⇢

Smart actions

If an entry looks like a URL, email, or phone number, an inline action appears to open it directly.

◧

Saved snippets

Hand-curate reusable text (email, phone, address) above the auto-history. Snippets never expire.

⌕

Live search

Type to filter. Subtitle shows "N of M" when filtering. Empty state explains zero matches versus zero copies.

⊘

No banners

No analytics, no third-party SDKs, no network requests for your data. The clipboard never leaves your phone.

How to play

From cold-start to good-at-it

A few short steps. The app gets out of your way after that.

01

Open Copy History

Keep the app open while you work. Every new copy is captured into the list automatically.

02

Tap to recopy

Any entry can be tapped to put it back on the clipboard. Re-copies do not create a duplicate entry.

03

Pin or save

Pin frequent items with ★. For frequently-typed text, tap + Saved to make a labeled snippet.

04

Search when it gets long

Use the search field to filter to anything that contains your query. Clear it to see everything again.
