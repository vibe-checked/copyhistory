Support

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

If your question isn't here, open an issue on GitHub. Public, traceable, and the same channel I use for everything else.

Why does iOS show a 'pasted from' banner?

iOS shows that banner whenever any app reads the clipboard. It's an iOS privacy feature — Copy History can't disable it. You can stop the banners by going to Settings → Apps → Copy History → Paste from Other Apps → Allow.

Why aren't copies captured when the app is closed?

iOS doesn't allow apps to read the clipboard in the background. Copy History only captures while the app is in the foreground. Bring the app to the front when you want to save something new.

Where is my history stored?

Everything lives on this device only, in the app's sandboxed local storage (React Native AsyncStorage). Nothing is sent to a server, nothing syncs to iCloud, nothing is shared with third parties.

What's the difference between History and Saved?

History is auto-captured and capped at 500 entries (oldest get evicted). Saved snippets are entries you create manually with a label — they live forever until you delete them.

How do I delete everything?

Tap Clear in the top right of the list, or uninstall the app to remove all stored data.

Why does my entry suddenly disappear?

If you've reached the 500-entry cap, new copies push the oldest unpinned ones off. Pin an entry to keep it forever; or save it as a snippet for the strongest guarantee.
